Overview

Accessible Quran ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Accessible Quran does not:

  • Collect personal data
  • Use analytics or tracking
  • Display advertisements
  • Require user accounts or login
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • Share data with third parties

Local Data Storage

The only data stored by the app is:

  • Bookmarks: Your saved bookmarks are stored locally on your device using iOS SwiftData framework
  • User Preferences: Settings like selected reciter and font size are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not backed up to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • Can be deleted by deleting the app

Third-Party Services

To provide Quran content, the app connects to these trusted Islamic content providers:

1. AlQuran.cloud

  • Purpose: Provides Quran text and English translations
  • What's sent: Surah numbers only
  • Website: alquran.cloud

2. MP3Quran.net

  • Purpose: Provides audio files for complete Surah recitations
  • What's sent: Surah numbers and reciter identifiers
  • Website: mp3quran.net

3. EveryAyah.com

  • Purpose: Provides audio files for individual verse recitations
  • What's sent: Surah and verse numbers, reciter identifiers
  • Website: everyayah.com

These services may log IP addresses and access times as part of normal web server operation. Please review their privacy policies for more information.

Network Access

The app requires internet connection to:

  • Download Quran text and translations
  • Stream audio recitations

All connections use HTTPS for security.

Children's Privacy

Accessible Quran does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed to be family-friendly and appropriate for all ages.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's nothing for us to:

  • Access
  • Modify
  • Delete
  • Export

Your bookmarks are stored on your device and can be managed within the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Security

While we don't collect data, we ensure:

  • All API connections use HTTPS encryption
  • Local data is protected by iOS security features
  • No data transmission to remote servers (except third-party Quran content providers)

Compliance

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

Since we do not collect personal information, CCPA rights do not apply.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with GDPR by:

  • Not collecting personal data
  • Not processing user information
  • Not transferring data to third parties
  • Not using cookies or tracking technologies
